Poodle!

If you've seen my picture already, you must've noticed that I'm a poodle. More accurately, Toy Poodle.

Cambridge Dictionary defines us as :

"a dog with curly hair that is usually cut short, except on its head, tail, and legs"

uhhhhh... Excuse me! We are much more than that!
